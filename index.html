Wenhan Yang (杨文瀚)


He received his PhD (Sep, 2018) in Computer Application Technology from Peking University. From September 2012 – September 2018, he was a Ph.D. candidate in Institute of Computer Science & Technology of Peking University (ICST). He was a member of Spatial and Temporal Restoration Understanding and Compression Team (STRUCT) at ICST. [CV]

Highlights

  • [Announcement] 03/2019, I still noticed that, some recent papers mention that, Rain100L and Rain100H include some overlapped backgournd images or wrong number of images. I need to mention that, the datasets online before Nov. 2018 might be not the correct one. I have updated the correct one in the project link after that. There are no backgournd images overlapped in the training and testing sets. The numbers of training images in Rain100L and Rain100H are both 1800. Thanks for my colleages (Xia Li at PKU and Renjie Wan at NTU) helping me correct the issue. If you have used the datasets online before August 2018, I think it is a reasonable remedy to manually screen out the overlapped background images as Dongwei Ren does in PReNet. If you have still questions or need help, please do not hesitate to contact with me.
